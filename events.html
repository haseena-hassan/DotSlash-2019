CODE FACEOFF


Hey folks!
Do you think your mind is fast enough to come up with a solution? Are you ready to test your skills among other people like you?

DotSlash '19 is giving you an opportunity to prove yourself with Code FaceOff. Here, you'll compete with another solo opponent and both will have to come up with a solution to the same problem. The fastest one to solve the problem will advance to the next stage. So gear up and code your way to the top.

Prize Worth 6k

Rules :
1. The players will have to play solo.
2. Programming language of choice can be used to solve the problem.
3. Coding is done offline on the systems provided.
4. All the test cases must be satisfied in order to qualify
5. Decisions of the coordinators are final

Registration fee: 100 per head

PAIR CODING


Hello peeps! Ever wondered how much your aptitude for coding can be assessed when you are part of a team ?
Well, DotSlash'19 is here with Pair Coding.
Pair Coding tests your expertise in coding as well as your cooperation skills.
The competition includes teams of two competing with one team member coding at a time. After a fixed interval of time, the other member resumes with the code.
So come along in teams of two and seize this compelling opportunity.

Prizes Worth 10k

Rules:
1. Teams of two.
2. There will be two rounds.
3. Rules of each round will be announced during the event.
4. Coding will be done offline on the systems provided.
5. Decisions of the coordinators are final

Registration fee: 150 per team.

Date : 27 April 2019

Venue:Department of Computer Science, CET
